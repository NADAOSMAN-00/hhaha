1st Recipe:Cookies

  • Time: 1 Hr 30 Min
  • Serves: 48
Ingredients Quantity
Eggs One
Butter 1 cup
Vanilla 1 teaspoon
Brown sugar 3/4 cup

Steps:

  1. Sift Dry Ingredients
  2. Cream Butter and Sugar.
  3. Beat in Eggs
  4. Add Dry Ingredients to Wet
  5. Bake and Cool

2nd Recipe:Cake

  • Time: 45 Min
  • Serves: 6
Ingredients Quantity
Eggs Sex
Butter 2 cups
Vanilla 1 teaspoon
sugar 3/4 cup

Steps:

  1. Prepare Baking Pans
  2. Allow Ingredients to Reach Room Temperature
  3. Preheat the Oven.
  4. Combine the Butter and Sugar.
  5. Pour Batter into Pans and Bake

3rd Recipe:Cupcake

  • Time:1 Hr 25 Min
  • Serves: 18
Ingredients Quantity
Eggs Two
Water 3/4 cups
salt 1/2 teaspoon
sugar 1/2 cup

Steps:

  1. Prepare Baking Pans
  2. Allow Ingredients to Reach Room Temperature
  3. Preheat the Oven.
  4. Combine the Butter and Sugar.
  5. Add Eggs One at a Time.
  6. Alternate Adding Dry and Wet Ingredients.
  7. Pour Batter into Pans and Bake

Source: Better Homes & Gardens
